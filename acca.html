Quantitative Aptitude:

Arithmetic:

Questions on percentages, profit and loss, ratio and proportion, time and work, time and distance, etc.

Algebra:

Questions on equations, inequalities, exponents, factorization, etc.

Geometry:

Questions on areas, volumes, angles, triangles, circles, etc.

Number Systems:

Questions on divisibility, prime numbers, LCM and HCF, fractions, etc.

1. Quantitative Aptitude:

a) If a shirt originally costs $40 and is discounted by 20%, what is the final price?

b) A train travels 300 km in 4 hours. What is its average speed in km/hr?

c) If \(2x + 5 = 17\), what is the value of \(x\)?

d) The area of a rectangle is 48 square meters, and its length is 12 meters. What is its width?

Logical Reasoning:

Deductive Reasoning:

Questions involving syllogisms, logical deductions, and conclusions.

Inductive Reasoning:

Questions based on patterns, analogies, and series completion.

Critical Reasoning:

Questions that assess your ability to evaluate arguments, draw conclusions, and identify assumptions.

Analytical Reasoning:

Questions on puzzles, seating arrangement, blood relations, etc.
