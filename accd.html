Accenture Interview Questions by Domain

Software Development

Technical questions for software development roles often focus on programming, algorithms, data structures, and software design principles. Here are some examples:

  • Explain the OOP concepts in detail.
  • What is the difference between overloading and overriding?
  • Describe the different types of joins in SQL.
  • What is normalization? Explain its different forms.
  • Explain the lifecycle of a servlet.
  • What are the main features of Java 8?
  • How does garbage collection work in Java?
  • Explain the difference between an abstract class and an interface.
  • How would you optimize a slow-running query?
  • Describe the Model-View-Controller (MVC) architecture.

Data Science

Questions for data science roles focus on statistics, machine learning, data analysis, and programming. Here are some examples:

  • Explain the bias-variance tradeoff in machine learning.
  • What is the difference between supervised and unsupervised learning?
  • Describe how a random forest algorithm works.
  • What are the assumptions of linear regression?
  • Explain the concept of p-value in hypothesis testing.
  • How do you handle missing data in a dataset?
  • What is the difference between classification and regression?
  • Explain the concept of clustering and its types.
  • Describe a situation where you used data analysis to solve a problem.
  • What are the key steps in a data science project?

Cloud Computing

Technical questions for cloud computing roles focus on cloud services, architecture, and security. Here are some examples:

  • What are the different types of cloud services (IaaS, PaaS, SaaS)?
  • Explain the benefits of using cloud computing.
  • What is the difference between public, private, and hybrid clouds?
  • How does AWS differ from Azure and Google Cloud Platform?
  • Explain the concept of auto-scaling in cloud environments.
  • What are cloud deployment models?
  • Describe the security challenges in cloud computing.
  • What is serverless computing and its advantages?
  • Explain the use of containers and Docker in cloud environments.
  • How do you implement a disaster recovery plan in the cloud?

Cybersecurity

Technical questions for cybersecurity roles focus on security protocols, threat management, and compliance. Here are some examples:

  • What are the different types of cybersecurity threats?
  • Explain the concept of encryption and its types.
  • What is a firewall and how does it work?
  • Describe the difference between symmetric and asymmetric encryption.
  • What are the key principles of information security?
  • How do you conduct a vulnerability assessment?
  • Explain the concept of intrusion detection and prevention systems.
  • What is the importance of multi-factor authentication?
  • Describe a situation where you dealt with a security breach.
  • What are the common compliance standards in cybersecurity?

Business Analysis

Questions for business analysis roles focus on understanding business processes, requirements gathering, and project management. Here are some examples:

  • What are the key responsibilities of a business analyst?
  • Explain the concept of SWOT analysis.
  • How do you gather requirements from stakeholders?
  • Describe a situation where you had to manage conflicting requirements.
  • What are the steps involved in creating a business case?
  • How do you prioritize requirements in a project?
  • Explain the difference between functional and non-functional requirements.
  • What are the common techniques used in business process modeling?
  • Describe a successful project you worked on as a business analyst.
  • How do you ensure that the delivered solution meets the business requirements?

DevOps

Questions for DevOps roles focus on continuous integration/continuous deployment (CI/CD), automation, and infrastructure as code. Here are some examples:

  • What is DevOps and why is it important?
  • Explain the CI/CD pipeline and its components.
  • How do you implement infrastructure as code (IaC)?
  • What are some popular DevOps tools and how are they used?
  • Explain the concept of containerization and its benefits.
  • Describe how you would automate a deployment process.
  • What is continuous testing and why is it important?
  • Explain the role of monitoring and logging in DevOps.
  • How do you manage configuration in a DevOps environment?
  • Describe a challenging DevOps project you worked on and how you overcame the challenges.
