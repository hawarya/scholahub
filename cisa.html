APTITUDE TEST QUESTIONS

1. Quantitative Aptitude

Arithmetic:

1. A man spends 75% of his income. His income is increased by 20% and his expenditure also increases by 10%. Find the percentage increase in his savings.

2. A train covers a distance of 600 km at a constant speed. If the speed of the train is increased by 20 km/hr, the journey would take 2 hours less. Find the original speed of the train.

3. Two pipes A and B can fill a tank in 20 hours and 30 hours respectively. Both pipes are opened together, but after 6 hours, pipe B is closed. How much time will pipe A take to fill the remaining tank?

Algebra:

1. Solve the quadratic equation: xÂ² - 7x + 12 = 0.

2. If the sum of the squares of two numbers is 97 and the square of their difference is 25, find the product of the two numbers.

3. A and B can do a piece of work in 12 days, B and C in 15 days, and C and A in 20 days. In how many days can A, B, and C together do the work?

Geometry:

1. The radius of a circular field is 14 meters. Calculate the area of the field.

2. A rectangle has a length of 12 cm and a breadth of 8 cm. Find its diagonal length.

3. In a right-angled triangle, the hypotenuse is 13 cm and one of the legs is 5 cm. Find the length of the other leg.

2. Logical Reasoning

Analytical Reasoning:

1. In a certain code language, 'ROAD' is written as 'URDG'. How is 'FIRM' written in that code?

2. A is the sister of B. B is married to C. C is the son of D. E is the father of D. How is E related to A?

3. Five friends P, Q, R, S, and T are sitting in a row. P is to the right of Q and T is to the left of R. S is at the end. Who is in the middle?

Pattern Recognition:

1. Find the next number in the series: 2, 6, 12, 20, 30, ?

2. Which one of the following does not belong to the group? 5, 15, 35, 45, 55

3. If 'LION' is coded as 'MPJO', how will 'TIGER' be coded in the same code?

Data Interpretation:

1. The bar chart below shows the sales of a company in four quarters of a year. Analyze the data and answer the following questions:

  • In which quarter was the sales highest?
  • Calculate the percentage increase in sales from Q1 to Q4.
  • What was the average sales over the four quarters?

2. A pie chart represents the distribution of expenditure in a household. If the total expenditure is $5000, how much is spent on Rent if it accounts for 20% of the total expenditure?

3. Analyze the line graph representing the growth of two companies over five years. Which company had a more consistent growth rate?

3. Verbal Ability

Reading Comprehension:

1. Read the following passage and answer the questions that follow:

The internet has transformed the way we communicate, learn, and do business. With the advent of social media, the speed and reach of information dissemination have exponentially increased. However, this also raises concerns about privacy and the authenticity of information shared online.
  • What are the positive impacts of the internet mentioned in the passage?
  • What concerns are raised about the internet in the passage?
  • What is the main idea of the passage?

Sentence Correction:

1. Identify the error in the following sentence: "Each of the players have their own locker."

  • b) are

  • d) have been

3. Correct the following sentence: "The committee discussed about the new policy for hours."
