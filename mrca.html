Mr. Cooper Placement Guide

APTITUDE TEST QUESTIONS

1. Quantitative Aptitude

Arithmetic:

1. A train travels at a speed of 90 km/h for 3 hours. How far does it travel?

2. If the cost price of an article is $200 and it is sold at a profit of 25%, what is the selling price?

3. A can complete a piece of work in 12 days, B can complete the same work in 18 days. In how many days will they complete the work together?

Algebra:

1. Solve the equation: 3x - 5 = 10.

2. If the sum of two numbers is 35 and their difference is 15, find the numbers.

3. The age of a father is twice the age of his son. If the sum of their ages is 54 years, find their ages.

Geometry:

1. Calculate the area of a circle with radius 7 cm.

2. Find the perimeter of a rectangle with length 10 cm and width 5 cm.

3. In a triangle, if two sides are 8 cm and 10 cm, and the included angle is 60 degrees, find the length of the third side.

2. Logical Reasoning

Analytical Reasoning:

1. If Monday is the first day of the month, which day will be the fourth Friday of the month?

2. If A is taller than B and C is taller than A, who is the tallest?

3. If all cups are plates and some plates are bowls, then some bowls are definitely cups. (True/False)

Pattern Recognition:

1. Find the next number in the series: 2, 6, 12, 20, 30, ?

2. Which one of the following does not belong to the group? 5, 15, 35, 45, 55

3. If 'APPLE' is coded as 'ELPPA', how is 'ORANGE' coded?

Data Interpretation:

1. The line chart below shows the sales of a company over 6 months. Analyze the data and answer the following questions:

  • What was the total sales in June?
  • Calculate the average sales over the 6 months.
  • In which month was the percentage increase in sales the highest?

2. A bar chart represents the distribution of students' scores in a class. If the total number of students is 50, how many students scored less than 60 marks?

3. Analyze the pie chart representing the allocation of expenses in a budget. What percentage of the budget is allocated to education?

3. Verbal Ability

Reading Comprehension:

1. Read the following passage and answer the questions that follow:

"Success is not the key to happiness. Happiness is the key to success. If you love what you are doing, you will be successful."
  • What is the main idea of the passage?
  • What does the passage suggest about the relationship between success and happiness?
  • What is the significance of loving what you do, according to the passage?

Sentence Correction:

1. Identify the error in the following sentence: "Each of the boys has done their homework."

2. Choose the correct option to complete the sentence: "Neither the manager nor his assistant ___ able to attend the meeting."

  • a) is
  • b) are
  • c) were
  • d) have been

3. Correct the following sentence: "The committee discussed about the new policy for hours."

Vocabulary:

1. Choose the word that is most similar in meaning to "abate":

  • a) increase
  • b) decrease
  • c) maintain
  • d) stop

2. Select the word that is opposite in meaning to "adversity":

  • a) misfortune
  • b) prosperity
  • c) hardship
  • d) difficulty

3. Fill in the blank with the appropriate word: "The scientist's ___ led to a groundbreaking discovery."

  • a) perseverance
  • b) lethargy
  • c) indifference
  • d) reluctance
