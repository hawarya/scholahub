Quantitative Aptitude:

1. What is the average of the first 50 natural numbers?

2. A train covers a distance of 600 km in 8 hours. What is the speed of the train?

3. If the cost price of an item is $50 and the selling price is $75, what is the profit percentage?

Logical Reasoning:

1. If all cups are plates and some plates are spoons, then some spoons are cups. True or False?

2. Complete the series: 2, 5, 10, 17, ___

3. If A + B means A is the brother of B, A - B means A is the sister of B, and A * B means A is the father of B, then what does P * Q + R - S mean?
